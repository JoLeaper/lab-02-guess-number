Number Guessing!

How To Play:

  • I am going to think of a number between 1 and 20. It'll be your job to guess it.
  • You have four chances. After each guess, I'll tell you if the number I'm thinking of is higher or lower.
  • If you guess the number right, you win! If not, you lose!
